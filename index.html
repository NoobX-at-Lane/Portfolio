How to make Serbian stuffed peppers

Serbian stuffed peppers are very commen in the balkans. However, in different places come different variations.

This version of stuffed peppers are very common in the central part of Serbia (the Sumadjia region to be exact).

However,No one knows where exactly stuffed peppers came to the balkans*

By the end of the day, you should be able to make your very own Serbian stuffed peppers.

To begin you will need the following items

  • green or red bell peppers
  • 1 pound of meat or other protien for the stuffing
  • 2 onions
  • 1/2 cup of rice
  • 1 tsp of dried parsley
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of pepper powder
  • 3 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of oil
  • 2 tomatos
  • 1 cup of water

Cooking time

  1. In a pan add oil and saut√© the onions
  2. Add meat and cook it for about 15 minutes.
  3. Grind a carrot and add it to the meat, then add washed rice and spices.
  4. Mix well and remove from the heat.
  5. Hollow out the peppers then fill about 2/3 of the pepper with the filling.
  6. Slice the tomatoes. Using the tip of the sliced tomato, cover the bell peppers.
  7. In a casserole dish, arrange the peppers, add the rest of the tomatoes and add the water.
  8. Cook them for about an hour then bake them for 15 minutes
